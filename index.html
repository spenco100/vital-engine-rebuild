On November 25th, 2020, Governor Kay Ivey officially funded a rural healthcare initiative, granting all rural hospitals and health clinics in Alabama, as well as all nursing homes in the state, access to a state-wide collaboration platform called VitalEngine.

VitalEngine is a HIPAA compliant Care Coordination tool that allows your facility to collaborate with ANY other healthcare professional or healthcare facility in the state of Alabama. You can utilize VitalEngine from any computer, tablet, or smartphone.
